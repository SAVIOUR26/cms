Makerere University Launches New Innovation Hub

By Sarah Mukasa 5 min read Oct 27, 2025

Kampala - Makerere University has officially opened its state-of-the-art Innovation and Technology Hub, marking a significant milestone in Uganda's journey towards becoming a technology-driven economy.

The new facility, located at the heart of the main campus, spans over 2,000 square meters and features cutting-edge equipment including 3D printers, robotics labs, and AI development stations.

"This hub represents our commitment to nurturing the next generation of African innovators and tech leaders," said Prof. Barnabas Nawangwe, Vice Chancellor.

The hub will serve as a collaborative space for students, faculty, and industry partners to work on groundbreaking projects.
