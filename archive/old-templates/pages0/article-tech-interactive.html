Uganda's First 5G Network Goes Live in Kampala

Breaking: MTN Uganda has officially launched the country's first commercial 5G network, covering major areas of Kampala including the CBD and Nakasero.

Download speeds of up to 1Gbps have been recorded in initial tests, making Uganda one of the early 5G adopters in East Africa.
