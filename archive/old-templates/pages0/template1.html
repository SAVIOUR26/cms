KAMPALA - Makerere University has unveiled East Africa's most advanced artificial intelligence research laboratory, positioning Uganda as a regional tech hub.

The Shs 2.5 billion facility features cutting-edge computing infrastructure and will focus on AI applications in agriculture, healthcare, and education.

"This marks a new chapter in Uganda's tech revolution" - Prof. Barnabas Nawangwe

The lab will collaborate with international tech giants and train 500 students annually in machine learning and data science.
