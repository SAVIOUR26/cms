Words to lift your spirit

Every dream begins with a single step. Every mountain is climbed one stride at a time. Today's poem reminds us that persistence, patience, and faith in the journey will always lead us to the summit.

Climb 'Til Your Dreams Come True

by Helen Steiner Rice

Often your tasks will be many,
And more than you think you can do...
Often the road will be rugged
And the hills insurmountable, too...
But always remember, the hills ahead
Are never as steep as they seem,
And with Faith in your heart start upward
And climb 'til you reach your dream,
For nothing in life that is worthy
Is ever too hard to achieve
If you have the courage to try it
And you have the Faith to believe...
For Faith is a force that is greater
Than knowledge or power or skill
And many defeats turn to triumph
If you trust in God's wisdom and will...
For Faith is a mover of mountains,
There's nothing that God cannot do,
So start out today with Faith in your heart
And "Climb 'Til Your Dreams Come True!"
Today's Reflection
Whether you're a student facing exams, an entrepreneur building your startup, or a professional climbing the career ladder—remember: the mountain always looks steeper from the bottom. But every successful person you admire once stood exactly where you are now, looking up at their own impossible dream.
"The hills ahead are never as steep as they seem... start upward and climb 'til you reach your dream."
