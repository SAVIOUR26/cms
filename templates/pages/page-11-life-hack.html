The 2-Minute Rule
Beat procrastination & boost productivity instantly
Ever notice how the hardest part of any task is just starting? Your brain resists big tasks but loves quick wins. Here's the hack that changed everything:
1
If a task takes less than 2 minutes, do it NOW. Reply to that email. Make your bed. Send that message. No thinking, just action.
2
For bigger tasks, commit to just 2 minutes. Tell yourself: "I'll study for just 2 minutes." Once you start, momentum takes over and you'll likely continue.
3
Stack your habits. After a 2-minute task, add another. "After I make my bed, I'll do 2 minutes of stretching." Small wins compound.
Pro Tip
Set a timer on your phone. When it goes off, ask: "What can I knock out in 2 minutes right now?" You'll be shocked how much gets done in a week.
