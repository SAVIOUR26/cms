Uganda Launches New Digital ID System

Government rolls out blockchain-based identification system across all 135 districts by Q2 2025

Kampala Pilot Success
Over 500,000 residents in Kampala have successfully enrolled in the first month of the digital ID rollout. The system has processed applications 5x faster than the traditional method.
2 hours ago
Advanced Security Features
The new ID includes biometric data, blockchain verification, and works as a digital wallet for government services including tax payments and licensing.
4 hours ago
Nationwide Rollout Plan
All 135 districts expected to have enrollment centers by Q2 2025. Mobile enrollment units will visit rural areas to ensure universal coverage.
6 hours ago
Regional Integration
The system is compatible with East African Community standards, enabling seamless cross-border verification for trade and travel.
